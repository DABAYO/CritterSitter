Get started

1 - Pet Guardians may search for potential Critter Sitters.
2 - Pet Guardians may post a classified to have a Critter Sitters contact you.
3 - Prospective Critter Sitters can post their experience and availability.
4 - Critter Sitters can search for jobs posted by Pet Guardians

How It works

If you're looking for a Critter Sitter, search our database to find the right person with the right experience. Once you think you have found a Critter Sitter that would be a good fit, send the candidate an introductory email explaining your needs and the needs of your pet. Or, if your prfer, you can post a classified and let prospective Critter Sitters in the area contact you. If you are a prospective Critter Sitter, tell us about your animal care experience, your location, and your email address so that Pet Guardians will be able to search for you. Or, you can answer a classified directly to search for clients.
